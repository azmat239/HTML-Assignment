Kashmir, region of the northwestern Indian subcontinent. It is bounded by the Uygur Autonomous Region of to the northeast and the Tibet Autonomous Region to the east (both parts of China), by the Indian states of Pradesh and Punjab to the south, by Pakistan to the west, and by Afghanistan to the northwest.

The people in the Jammu area are Muslim in the west and Hindu in the east and speak Hindi, Punjabi, and Dogri. The inhabitants of the Vale of Kashmir and Pakistani areas are mostly Muslim and speak Urdu and Kashmiri.
